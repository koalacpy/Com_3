Practice

In each trial, you will be shown four images. After a couple seconds have passed, a 'Go' button will appear near the bottom of your screen. When you click the 'Go' button, audio instructions will begin to play.

Try to remember three main rules:

1: Try to keep your mouse still immediately after clicking 'Go'.

2: Move your mouse as soon as you have a hunch or vague idea about which object you're supposed to click (but not before then).

3: If the audio finishes and you haven't moved your mouse yet, you're too slow!

The timing of this experiment can be tricky at first, and that's okay! You will receive feedback if you're moving your mouse too fast or too slow.

We'll start with a few practice trials so you can get used to the rhythm. Click 'Start' when you're ready!
