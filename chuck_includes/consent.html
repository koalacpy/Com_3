Consent Statement

It is highly recommended that you complete this task in Firefox or Google Chrome. Safari and Internet Explorer may not be fully supported.

Description:  You are invited to participate in an approximately 20 minute research study investigating how people communicate, reason, and understand language. Your participation in this survey is anonymous: researchers will not receive any identifying information about you.

Risks and Benefits of Participation: Risks involved in this study are the same as those normally associated with using a computer. We cannot and do not guarantee or promise that you will receive any benefits from this study, though there may be benefits at a societal level from the knowledge gained regarding how people communicate, reason, and understand language.

Participation from this point is voluntary; refusal to participate in the experiment will involve no penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.
